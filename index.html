1. Scope

This Policy applies to the iCard System (HNA iCard) application and related backend services required to provide core activation and BLE reader connectivity features.

2. Information We Process

2.1 Permissions and On-Device Data

  • Camera access to scan activation QR codes.
  • Bluetooth access to discover and connect supported RFID BLE readers.
  • NFC access to read from or write secure commands to supported RFID/NFC tags.
  • Photo library access when you explicitly select an image for QR scanning.
  • Local configuration/runtime data such as server settings, connection history, activation/runtime records, policy snapshots, cryptographic credentials, and app preferences stored on your device.

2.2 Network Data

To provide app functionality, the App transmits required activation and device-binding information to backend endpoints over secure transport in production environments. Activation requests may include information entered by you, device and application information, QR data, and cryptographic public-key material. Sensitive activation request payloads are encrypted in transit in addition to the production HTTPS transport.

3. How We Use Information

  • Activate devices through QR-based workflows.
  • Associate a license/card with the activation information and device used during activation.
  • Establish and maintain BLE reader sessions.
  • Perform supported NFC/RFID card operations.
  • Support policy-controlled offline operation.
  • Persist necessary app state and user configuration.
  • Prevent duplicate or unauthorized activation, perform revocation checks, and review activation issues.
  • Improve reliability, provide customer support, diagnose operational errors, and maintain service security.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your jurisdiction, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Consent (for device permissions such as camera/Bluetooth/photos).
  • Performance of a contract/service (to deliver core app functionality).
  • Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, service integrity).
  • Legal obligations (where required by applicable law).

5. Sharing and Recipients

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only where necessary to operate core app functionality, including backend infrastructure and service providers acting on our instructions.

Activation records may be made available to the organization or customer that issued or manages the relevant license, and to authorized administrators or support personnel, subject to role, permission, and customer-scope controls. These recipients may use the information for license administration, access or parking operations, activation review, and support.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs for behavioral advertising within this App.

6. Data Retention

On-device data is retained until you uninstall the App or clear app/device data (where supported). Server-side data is retained only for as long as needed for legitimate operational/legal purposes and then deleted or anonymized according to internal policies.

Removing an activated card, clearing app storage, or uninstalling the App removes applicable on-device data but does not automatically delete activation records already stored by the backend. To request access, correction, or deletion of eligible backend records, use the process described in Section 8.1.

7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational controls designed to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continuously improve safeguards appropriate to risk.

Controls include secure production transport, encrypted activation request payloads, cryptographic device binding, platform secure storage for activation credentials, and role/permission/customer-scope controls for authorized backend access.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent for permission-based processing.
  • Request portability where legally applicable.

You can also manage device permissions at any time in Android/iOS settings. If required permissions are denied, some features may not function.

You may delete applicable on-device activation and connection information using available controls in the App. This does not automatically remove corresponding backend activation records; contact us to exercise rights relating to backend data.

8.1 Rights Request Process

Submission channel: support@hna.id.vn

Email subject: Privacy Request - HNA iCard

Minimum details: account/contact identifier, request type, and supporting context.

Verification: we may request additional information to verify request ownership.

Response timeline: initial response within 7 business days, final handling target within 30 days (unless law requires otherwise).

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the legal age in relevant jurisdictions. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

10. International Transfers

If data is processed outside your country, we apply safeguards required under applicable law to protect transferred information.
